Short History of Calculators

The earliest calculating device was the abacus, used thousands of years ago. Later came mechanical calculators in the 1600s, followed by electronic calculators in the 20th century. Modern calculators are digital, fast, compact, and extremely accurate.

Short History of Computing Machines

Early computing machines were huge and slow, built with gears and mechanical components. Over time, vacuum tube computers arrived, then transistor-based systems, and eventually microprocessors—making today’s digital devices possible.
